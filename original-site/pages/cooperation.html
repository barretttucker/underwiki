cooperation

Many outsiders think that the Dark Elves can't cooperate, but that is far from the truth. Each of the Chosen recognize that it is in their best interests to work together. Decades of training together and carefully planned coordination allows Dark Elf successes where more individualistic societies might fail.

As a simple example, Dark Elf ambushes rarely leave survivors - every contingency has been foreseen and accounted for, with each member of the ambush party carrying out their role perfectly.

This strong ability to cooperate does not preclude intense competition, which is encouraged by the third Rule of the moral code.
