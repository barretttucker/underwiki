T'lindeth

T'lindeth is one of the two major outposts adjacent to Dolthgurdar, and as such controls the flow of goods from the south-western outposts beyond to the Dolthgurdar marketplace. The traffic is not as great or as rich as that through Dusklyngh, but control of T'lindeth has nevertheless proved highly profitable for its new stewards House Kilrahel.

T'lindeth produces delicacy food items (they are the only producer of the succulent Jil'kin mushroom) in addition to being located strategically. Until they were eliminated by Kilrahel, T'lindeth was the historic center of House Kilani's power.
