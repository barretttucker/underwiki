darksight

Dark Elves can see in the complete darkness of underground passages and chambers; the range of this sight is out to 120 feet. Darksight is based in the ability to distinguish between heat and cold. Printed materials are not legible with darksight - illumination is required to write or read such things.

When faced with actual illumination, Dark Elves require a short period of time (1-4 rounds) to readjust their vision before being able to see clearly. When using Darksight, they see in black and white; color is visible with illumination (typically from a candle, a lava flow or irridescent fungus).
