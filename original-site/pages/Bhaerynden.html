Bhaerynden

Lucrative goldmine and waypoint on the way from both Nerr'tenden and T'lethtyrr to Dolthgurdar. Outpost of House Keniryn and post of Illiam. Until recently traffic from Bhaerynden had been plagued by marauders in the catacombs separating it from Llurth Dreier. Caravan traffic has seemingly returned to normal, though several conspicuous non-merchant parties from Dolthgurdar have passed through on their way to Nerr'tenden in recent weeks.
