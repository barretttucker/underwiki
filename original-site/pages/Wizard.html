Wizard

There are some dark elf wizards, but dark elves seem to be naturally drawn to innate casting a la Sorceror. Being a wizard is tough for dark elves; their darksight doesn't allow them to read wizardly writing - they need to light candles to see, and nobody likes that. They also need to tote around scrolls, books, candles and flint. They're also 95% motivated by academic pursuits and research. They're not likely to go out adventuring. Besides, when it comes down to it, dark elves really are good at being sorcerors.
