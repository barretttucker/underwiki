artistic

Dark Elves admire things of beauty - objects, people, artistic creations, music, food and dance. In fact, no Dark Elf can reach adulthood without becoming proficient in at least one cultural art form. To fail to do this would be an insufferable humiliation to the House, and would leave the individual little better than a barbarian of a lesser race.
