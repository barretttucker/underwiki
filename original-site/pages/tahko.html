tahko

These huge drums are made from the skins of large animals, a true rarity in the darkelf environment. Precious and treasured, the Takho drums are played only by those trained in their use. They are usually only found in city Houses of some wealth. Having a skilled Tahko drummer is a mark of prestige in darkelf society.
