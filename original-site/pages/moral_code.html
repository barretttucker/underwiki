moral code

The Dark Elves have a very strong, very simple set of core rules that have helped them to survive, and even thrive, in the hostile and challenging world they inhabit. These rules are tightly bound into the racial identity of all Dark Elves, giving them a clear sense of community, sisterhood and superiority over all others.

The Rules:

  1. Do Nothing that Harms the Dark Elf Cause

  2. Serve the Dark Elf Race as You Serve Yourself

  3. Destroy or Dominate Weakness

There is usually but a single punishment for violation: Death. In lenient situations (or privately judged situations), the offender might merely be enslaved or indentured.

Any senior member of a Dark Elf's house has authority and is responsible for judging and meting out punishment. Only the City Council can judge a House Council.
