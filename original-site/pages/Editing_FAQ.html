Editing FAQ

Changing Stuff on this Site:

Creating your Account:

1. Go to http://underwiki.redwoodrhino.com/cgi-bin/moin-1.3.4.cgi/UserPreferences

2. Click 'Create Account' and not login the first time you enter the site, and must fill out all fields. On subsequent logins you need only fill in use and password and click login.

3. Use just your first name in proper case: Nate, Barrett (not nate, barrett)

Then you're in: You home page will be visible on the right as a link with your name.

There are a couple of things you can do to a page: edit one or create one.

To make a page private so only you (and the mighty Steve) can view or edit it, at the top of the page place: 

#acl YourName:read,write,admin,rename,delete
