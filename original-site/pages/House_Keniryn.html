House Keniryn

[Keniryn.gif]

House Keniryn is the 23rd house. Their family has been decimated in recent skirmishes with other Houses and even some infighting.

Keniryn distinguishes itself from other Middle Houses in two principal ways: First, their Drum and Dance troupes are famous in Dolthgurdar concert halls. Second, they have fostered intense protective loyalty from their soldiers toward the noble family: Keniryn's family number only six, nearly the smallest family in any House. This makes them an attractive target for other Houses looking for a step up.

Keniryn claims Bhaerynden as its outpost, a profitable gold mine distant from Dolthgurdar and in between Nerr'tenden and T'lethtyrr.
