Valron's Message

From Valron:

Respected Noble Mother,

As you must know, I have been employed for nearly 20 years as a guard in the service of House Oussund. Several months ago, my death was feigned while I was moved to a new, elite unit called the Spider Guard, and stationed in Nerr’tenden outpost.

Two days ago I met an intruder, an elf who recognized me and deferred to me as a noble of House Orlylyl. He was clearly a House Member who went by the name of Falt. Seeing him has brought my past into stark relief. Although I did not recognize him (and had to pretend to misunderstand him because of my companions), this elf can vouch for my identity.

Captain Xulleyl appears to be grooming our elite group for some kind of power play within House Oussund. You should know that your ‘expedition’ forces have been destroyed by the Spider Guard. Now that I have learned House Orlylyl is targeting Oussund aggressively, I cannot continue to let myself be used by her if it will mean that I must contest my own House.

I offer to act as an agent for House Orlylyl to aid in the operation to exploit House Oussund's mines. I bring the other extraordinary members of the small, elite Spider Guard with me, as they are eager to work for a House with great potential status.

I can arrange for the completion of the mission now as an insider, as other House Orlylyl efforts have failed.

Please let the Honored Matron know that she has a new weapon.

Your humble servant,

Valron’dar Orlylyl
