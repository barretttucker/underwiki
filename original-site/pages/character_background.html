character background

It is important that your character have a background. The history and experiences of your character will inform the decisions you make during character creation and then during each and every session.

The PC's in this game have been brought together to form an elite group called the Spider Guard. You have come from a variety of backgrounds - some possible ones might be:

  • a noteworthy slave

  • a captured member of another family

  • a very junior daughter or son of the family of House Oussund

  • a talented guard, sorceror, etc.

  • anything else that fits in with the world as outlined in this wiki site.

Feel free to put in story and character hooks for Steve to use if you wish to have more emphasis on your character during the campaign.

Make sure that you specifically address some experience having to do with one or more of the personality / social factors described in the Roleplaying a dark elf PC section.

Your Character Background can be as short or as long as you need it to be to address the above issues. If that means writing three sentences, so be it. If you want to write a novel describing your family's history and your tormented childhood (remember, childhood does last for decades), that's fine too.
