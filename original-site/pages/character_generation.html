character generation

1. Before you begin, read role-playing

2. Next, see the Dark Elf racial traits and dark elf and dark elf PC sections.

3. Email Steve with an outline of the character you'd like to play. Once he's ok'ed your idea:

4. You need to create your character sheet.

  • Note: for Gestalt characters, it looks like the easiest way to do this is simply print out the blank character sheets from Hero Forge and do it manually. Let me know if you need any help with it.

    If you're more daring (or have a single-class character) Hero Forge and Spell Forge spreadsheets or the PCGen application to create your character. Hero Forge is located in the Spider Guard Yahoo! group site (check the Monster Manual option on the first tab to enable Elf, Drow):

    • http://games.groups.yahoo.com/group/spiderguard/files/

      PCGen will be posted there soon.

      eTools is similar to PCGen and I'm having better luck getting the extended datasets. Try this for now.

      Additional character sheet options are listed on the Character Sheets page.

      Excel file Drow name generator: http://underwiki.redwoodrhino.com/downloads/Drow_names.xls

5. Make a cool looking character image (totally optional)

  • (for the artistically uninclined try: http://underwiki.redwoodrhino.com/downloads/HeroMachine2.zip)

For rolling stats:

  • Roll 4d6, drop the lowest die. Repeat five times so you have a list of six stat rolls. If you don't have at least one 16 (before racial modifications), reroll all six. Assign as you like to the various stats.

Starting Equipment:

  • Take what you think is reasonable starting equipment for your character, based on their level and background.

Make sure you look at the house rules section for legal texts, virtues and flaws and other custom rules.

5. Email a copy of the proposed character sheet to Steve for approval, along with your written character background.
