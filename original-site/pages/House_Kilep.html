House Kilep

[Kilep.gif]

House Kilep ('People of the Underdark') is the 47th House.

Kilep is a poor house, and its workers are focused on learning how to be efficient and capable tunnel runners, but the reality is that they are more often used as simple guards, shock troops and menial laborers by other houses.

Several months ago, they suffered a serious reputation setback when two caravans they were leading to House Oussund were lost en route, along with one of their few capable captains.
