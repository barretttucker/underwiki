bath master

Similar to tea masters, Bath Masters are experts in a critical social art - in this case, relaxation (instead of civility). A Bath Master and their staff are often trained as bodyguards in addition to their more expected tasks, as guests are more vulnerable in their care than at other times. They are also in charge of the bath house.
