house

A 'House' is the largest unit of social organization in dark elf society. Each House strives for dominance over its competitors in such a way that it doesn't break the moral code - or at least doesn't get caught doing so.

A House Name can also be used in place of a Family member's name. For example, any Family member of House Oussund could simply be referred to as 'Oussund'.

Hierarchy: Each House is ruled by the Matriarch and comprised of the family, the membership, the service and the guard.

Note that slaves and other Animals (like lizards, spiders and farmed water animals) are property.
