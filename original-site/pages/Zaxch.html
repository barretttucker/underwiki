Zaxch

Zacxh'leborin of the fallen house Leborin...

is one of the most fierce looking Elves you will ever meet. He stands 5 feet tall and is dressed in rags. His strong tough body is covered in so much scar tissue that it is difficult to find any uncut flesh. The stubble on his bald head suggest that if it were allowed to grow, his hair would only poke through the managarie of ruined flesh in patches. Yet despite all this there is a handsome and confident air about the dark elf. Obviously a slave, there is a vacantly defiant cast to his purple eyes.

At games start he wears a hodge podge of salvaged and bloody leather armor bits and two mismatched rapiers all covered in a dark lizardskin cloak.

He never looks anyone in the eye. As a good slave should not...

Conspicuously absent is the "Spidergaurd Pin." As are the orders he might cast about due to its possession.
