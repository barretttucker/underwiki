Wicked Courtesan

Members of this prestige class are dancing, mincing, simpering, uh ... women of the night. What the world does not see however, is the need to charm her way into your heart, uh literally. She'll try to cajole, control, manipulate or dominate you, and should those 'delicate' tactics fail, mince about and put a poisoned arrow through your heart. Take that cupid!. No sneak attacks in the dark for her. When you die it will be looking into her eyes, confused and surprised that such a cute simpering litte thing would do this to you, and should the courtesan be good enough, maybe even glad that their radiant face was the last thing you saw.

Based loosely on the Scout class and the Assassin Prestige class http://d20srd.org/srd/prestigeClasses/assassin.htm, see Substitutions from Assassin Base below.

Requirements

To qualify to become a wicked courtesan, a character must fulfill all the following criteria

Charisma 16+

Skills

Diplomacy 8 Ranks, Intimidate 8 ranks, Perform 5 ranks

Special

The character must sleep with someone just for the purpose of manipulating and betraying them later.

Class Skills

Balance (Dex), Bluff (Cha), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Decipher Script (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Disguise (Cha), Escape Artist (Dex), Forgery (Int), Gather Information (Cha), Hide (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Jump (Str), Listen (Wis), Move Silently (Dex), Open Lock (Dex), Perform (Cha), Search (Int), Sense Motive (Wis), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Spot (Wis), Swim (Str), Tumble (Dex), Use Magic Device (Cha), and Use Rope (Dex).

Skill Points at Each Level

8 + Int modifier.

Class Features

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Charmers gain no additional proficiency in any weapon or armor.

Poison Use

Dark Courtesans are trained in the use of poison and never risk accidentally poisoning themselves when sliding a poisoned needle into someone's wrist, or swabbing contact poison across the back of a lovers neck (you get the point), oh yeah and putting it on blades too, etc.

Charm Touch

If an Dark Coutesan studies her mark for 3 rounds and then touches her "new friend" they must make a Will save with a DC of 10 + the Dark Coutesans Level + the Dark Coutesan Cha modifier or become charmed as per the Charm Person spell. this effect is not subject to spell resistence as it an aspect of the Dark Coutesans charismatic persona. The mark may be engaged in combat, and so may the Dark Coutesan. The Dark Coutesan may not have attacked the mark during the 3 rounds of study, and must make a DC18 concentration check each round to maintain her study if engaged in combat. If she is touching the mark during combat she must make a successful touch attack. Once the Dark Coutesan has completed her 3 rounds of study she must touch the mark within 3 rounds or must begin again. The DC of this power is affected by How Charming class ability.

Spells

Beginning at 1st level, an Dark Coutesan gains the ability to cast a number of arcane spells. To cast a spell, an Dark Coutesan must have an Charisma score of at least 10 + the spell’s level. Dark Coutesan bonus spells are based on Charisma, and saving throws against these spells have a DC of 10 + spell level + the Dark Coutesan's Charisma bonus. When the Dark Coutesan gets 0 spells per day of a given spell level she gains only the bonus spells ahe would be entitled to based on his Charisma score for that spell level.

The Dark Coutesan's spell list appears below. A Dark Coutesan casts spells just as a bard does.

Upon reaching 6th level, at every even-numbered level after that (8th and 10th), a Dark Coutesan can choose to learn a new spell in place of one she already knows. The new spell’s level must be the same as that of the spell being exchanged, and it must be at least two levels lower than the highest-level Dark Coutesan spell the Dark Coutesan can cast. A Dark Coutesan may swap only a single spell at any given level, and must choose whether or not to swap the spell at the same time that she gains new spells known for that level.

How Charming (Ex)

Beginning at 2nd level, the Dark Coutesan becomes so charming, it becomes harder to resist his enchantment/telepathy spells/powers. The save DC for any enchantment/telepathy spell/power increases +1. This rises to +2 at 5th level and +3 at 8th level. These increases are cumulative with those granted by the Spell Focus and Greater Spell Focus feats, or their psionic equivalents.

Mincing Feet

Fleet of foot and light on her feet, the Dark Coutesan gains a bonus of +10ft to her movement.

Distracting Presence

Due to their alluring nature and swaying charming aspect, Dark Coutesans gain their Charisma bonus to their AC. They retain this even when they would normally loose their Dexterity bonus (e.g. when flat footed). They loose this bonus if they are concealed from the attacker or behind cover. They also loose this bouns if they should wear armor heavier than light.

Unfettered Feet

The Dark Coutesan's movements are too much to be contained by mortal means. She can easily slip out of bonds, grasping patrons, grapples and confining spells. This ability is per the Freedom of Movement spell. She looses this ability if wearing medium or heavy armor or is encumbered.

Wicked Courtesan Spell List

Wicked Courtesans choose their spells from the following list:

1st Level

comprehend languages, disguise self, detect poison, feather fall, ghost sound, obscuring mist, silent image, true strike.

2nd Level

alter self, cat’s grace, eagle’s splendor, enthrall, knock, invisibility, silence, tongues, touch of idiocy.

3rd Level

clairaudience/clairvoyance, invisibility sphere, major image, haste, charm monster, glibness.

4th Level

discern lies, dimension door, greater invisibility, locate creature, lesser geas, neutralize poison, poison, shadow conjuration.
