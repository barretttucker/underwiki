virtues and flaws

A number of games have Flaws and Virtues systems to help flesh out personality. Here's how it works:

Characters typically select one or more Virtues and an equal amount of Flaws at character generation. An example might be choosing "dazzling wit" as a Virtue and "self-absorbed" as a Flaw. Flaws and Virtues have in-game effects. In our example, the PC might get a +1 to Diplomacy and Intimidate checks (for the Virtue) and suffer a -1 penalty to Sense Motive or Gather Information checks (for the Flaw).

I like the idea and want to give the players a chance to have a bit more fun with their characters at the table. However, I don't want to add in pages and pages of Virtues and Flaws, so here's what we're going to do:

Make up your own Virtue and create a Flaw that is of equal impact. Describe them and email them to Steve; when it's approved, you can add it to the house virtues and flaws section of this site.
