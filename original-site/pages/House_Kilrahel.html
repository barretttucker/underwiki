House Kilrahel

[Kilrahel.gif]

House Kilrahel (the 'People of the Gods') has recently won the title of 13th House. House Kilrahel has toppled and apparently eradicated the long-standing rulers of T'lindeth, House Kilani.

Kilrahel's power comes from their well-trained Cadre and their priestesses; both groups are specially integrated with the Kilrahel army. Kilrahel is viewed by its new hierarchy neighbors as a sort of dangerous nouveau-riche; while well-armed, their cultural accomplishments are on par with the Minor Houses - the 'Tunnel Rat' houses!

The crown jewel of the Kilrahel possessions is their newly-acquired property T'lindeth, a mushroom farm with the powerful position of being the first outpost on the Southern Branch. Kilrahel also has an ancestral carving-mushroom plantation Moht'lethtyrr located far to the north, above Maeralyn.
