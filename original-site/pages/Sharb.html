Sharb

Leader by succession of the Catacomb Bandits. After their defeat at the hands of the Spider Guard, Nal recruited Sharb and her band to work in Nerr'tenden. She is currently tasked with enforcing security at the Spider Guard mining camp as they seek The Mineral.
