Hash'tal

Hash is the rogue trainer at Nerr'tenden. She is an insightful teacher with a quiet voice, but don't let that fool you--she brooks no lapses of concentration. Her grace and agility are astounding, giving her movements a near-supernatural quality.

Her body was returned to Nerr'tenden during the siege.
