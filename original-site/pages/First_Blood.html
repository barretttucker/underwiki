First Blood

The PC's all meet one another and share a meal in their "guest" quarters in the Nerr'tenden stronghold. The following morning, they are brought to the arena, where they meet Captain Xulleyl for the first time. She speaks for a bit about the promise of each of them, and that assembled they will form a great weapon for the House.

She soon gets down to business and the group is put into a winner-takes-all combat; in the end it comes down to Zacxh and Kadyel in a slugfest which Zacxh wins. The Captain gives him a Spider Guard pin and pronounces him leader of the group.

Upon return, the group rests for the rest of the day and the evening. The following morning, Inarra confronts the group with a licentious display of a shallow cut spanning her entire back from hip to shoulder. She informs the group that she knows the perpetrator and implies that she will have a similar rendezvous with them some evening.

For a detailed narrative from Zacxh's perspective, look at ChapterTwo.
