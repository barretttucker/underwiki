life at Nerr'tenden

Inhabitants at Nerr’tenden are relatively comfortable, considering the distance from Dolthgurdar. There is a small tea house and a modest but comfortable bath and spa chamber fed from the springs. The Steward has insisted upon maintaining the amenities of civilized life, with gatherings for music, sculpture and tea. Skilled cooks provide quality meals (for the dark elves), while the bath master relaxes the mind and body of Nerr’tenden’s residents with her ministrations and scented oils. While the accommodations are not luxurious by any stretch of the imagination, Nerr’tenden is a comfortable place to plot ascendance.
