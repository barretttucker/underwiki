role-playing

This campaign is going to be different than others you've taken part in. I want this to be a new experience for each of us. An important part of that is role-playing a character that is new to you. I imagine that few (if any) of you have ever roleplayed in a campaign comprised entirely of "evil" characters in an underground setting, so that is a start.

But I want to go further.

I want each and every one of you to pick a character type and personality that is new for you - that will be a challenge. It may be that you identify with the character, or that you don't. I imagine that over time, each of you will find something meaningful in your character that you can use to express yourself. Make it different. If you always play the water-spell mage, don't do it here. The same goes for personality - pick something new and interesting. This is your chance to broaden your horizons, to act as someone you never would dream of in the everyday roleplay.

The gauntlet has been thrown. Have fun!
