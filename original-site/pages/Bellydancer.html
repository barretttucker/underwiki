Class Features

Weapon and armor proficiency

The Bellydancer is proficient with all simple weapons, the scimitar, the finger cymbals, and the scarf.

+1 Existing casters level

For every level of belly dancer the belly dancer gains an additionally level in the spellcasting ability of one spellcasting class she had before becoming a belly dancer.

Tools of the trade

The scimitar, finger cymbals, and scarf become useful as performance equipment and battle weapons at first level. The Belly dancer is able to seductively dance and battle with her weapons simultaneously. The scimitar is used as a slashing weapon as are the finger cymbals (d4 damage, crit on 19-20). The scarf is useful as a five foot range disarming and tripping weapons with +2 to that task (an additional +4 if the belly dancer can beat her opponent in a contest of her perform versus their sense motive). and doesn’t draw an attack of opportunity. Furthermore a the belly dancer moves as a seductive and graceful dance for all of combat all of these weapons usages are not only instantly interchangeable (no need for an action for drawing a weapon), but seductive and alluring to boot.

Moves of the Trade

At second level the belly dancer has mastered being charming on stage and off. This gives their performances, spells, and all other action a level of charisma they didn’t have before. At level 2 the belly dancer gains the Alluring feat.

Armor of Cuteness

No warrior can stand against the belly dancer’s secret weapon, her smile. At third level this gives the belly dancer the ability to play upon this reluctance in combat, giving her a miscellaneous bonus to AC equal to her charisma bonus.

Dance magic

At fourth level the belly dancer’s spellcasting and performance ability merge. The dances of the belly dancer seem to become magical themselves, giving her a +2 innate modifier for perform checks, and giving her the disguise spell feat.

Gypsy Band

At fifth level the belly dancer has become such a performer that she attracts others to her cause, forming a roving band of gypsies, thieves, and musicians to serve as her companions, band, and fellow performers. The attracted followers may be of ill repute, but will fall within one adjustment of the belly dancer’s alignment, making them perhaps lovable scoundrels. Treat as Leadership, but without the advantage of a cohort.

Armor of Grace

No warrior’s sword can match the swift and graceful motions of a belly dancer’s most dangerous weapons, her hips. At level six the belly dancer has refined her combat dancing to the point where she can dodge, evade, and avoid attacks without breaking her all important rhythm. The belly dancing bard gains a dodge bonus to AC equal to her dexterity bonus.

Dance of the scimitars

At level 8 the belly dancer has mastered a traditional dance known by all master belly dancers, the dance of the scimitars. In this dance the belly dancer balances scimitars on various parts of her body to the amazement and chagrin of her enemies. To perform this dance the belly dancer must succeed in a DC 20 perform check and have two scimitars (or other bladed weapons of similar design [short swords, long swords all styled as scimitars). While performing this dance, the belly dancer fights with the two blades as if she had ambidexterity and two weapons fighting, but maintains a suitable amount of stylized combat sex-appeal.

Dance of the Alluring Blossom

At level 9 the bellydancer masters another dance. This dance woos her audience with its irresistible beat and seductive movements of the body. The bellydancer makes a perform check while doing this dance. All who look upon her must make a will save equal to half of the perform check result or be held fascinated for as long as the bellydancer continues to dance (this dance requires the bellydancer to exclusively do only this dance for the duration). Those who succeed the will save are dazed for one round as they take in her Beauty for just a moment before breaking its hold. This dance can be done once per day.

Dance of the Scorching Beauty

At level 10 the bellydancer has developed such an ability of merging high-speed hip movement into magical enticements she has mastered yet another dance. Like the supernaturally beautiful nymph the bellydancer can summon more beauty than mere mortals can behold. Once per day the bellydancer can perform a dance, the Dance of the scorching beauty, that will cause onlookers to die or go blind. To perform this dance the bellydancer makes a perform check. All people looking upon her at the time must make a fort save equal to half of that perform check or die. Those who save are blinded for 4d6 rounds.
