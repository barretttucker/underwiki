House Melemtor

[Melemtor.gif]

House Melemtor ('Mothers of the Abyss') is the 36th House.

Melemtor is a minor House that specializes in caravan transport for other Houses, and is known for its professionalism and efficiency.

The tiniest house of all Dolthgurdar, Melemtor is currently suffering under terrible leadership from its Matron; it is unclear is she is going senile or even insane. A coup from inside or outside must be imminent; the House cannot continue as it is.
