Chapter Eleven The bag.

Saner minds prevailed and the party rested for a few hours in the Mind flayer's lair. Zacxh normally would have volunteered for the first watch, but his head was pounding and his body ached in every joint. He was so mentally and emotionally exhausted by the furious mental battle he had fought with the mind flayers that he found himself slumping heavily on the ground and insensible in a restful trance before the rest of the party had even finished sorting out their gear from the fight. Zacxh was, as usual, physically uninjured by the battle, but his mind and spirit were ravaged. He had for the first time in memory found the absolute limit of his mental reserves. He sought his mental fortress to escape the ache in his bones but it was collapsed by fatigue. Zacxh had never experienced anything like it.

He woke from his dreamless trance to Inarra's voice as she plyingly discussed some matter of strategy with Nal. The day was in full bloom and the walls radiated a reassuringly warm glow. The radiance illuminated the lair in stark relief and Zacxh could see clearly his surroundings. The Lair... it all came back in a flash. The battle, his fatigue, all of it returned like a recollection from childhood. Well rested, Zacxh saw the incident as a cautionary yet somehow distant event. Don't underestimate the Illithid. They are deadly.

The Spiderguard formed up into their accustomed positions and moved out. Zacxh took point with Sorn and moved out to just within eyeshot. Zacxh practiced his silent movement but was nowhere as proficient in the art as the dancer with his nimble feet and absolute balance. Sorn could all but disappear at will, and Zacxh suspected that there was a bit more to it than mere stealth. The eerie little elf sometimes appeared to actually vanish when he was trying to go unseen. Zacxh wondered if that might be some latent ancestral ability which Sorn would one day discover.

The voyage to Karsolithuyl went quickly and without incident. The Spiderguard was met with only minimal resistance at the gate, though the outpost was well designed to repel attack. The guards demanded an explanation as to the Spiderguard’s purpose but Inarra used her considerable charms to seduce and beguile the pliant males into allowing entry. She promised them 'attention' after her bath. They provided enthusiastic escort.

The Karsolithuyl bathhouse put Nerr’tenden’s to shame. The water was fresher, better regulated for temperature and had not even a trace of the sulphuric smell which tainted the water in Nerr’tenden. The attendants were more civil and better skilled in the art and courtesies of the bath, and the chilled wine they brought was so delicious that even Zacxh did not abstain. This was an activity which nourished the spirit, and Zacxh emerged himself fully...

After the Spiderguard had settled into baths (Sorn took care but failed to be discreet about ogling Inarra as she disrobed for her soak), an authoritative female came to call. She defied good manners and what was in most places considered strict protocol by barging in fully armed and armored. She introduced herself as Ttt, the Captain of the Karsolithuyl guard and dismissed Zacxh with a typically female arrogance when he bluntly pointed out that she was really being quite rude and should disrobe and join them in the waters if she intended to visit. The combination of hot water, cool wine, and another supremely manipulative and condescending female put Zacxh into a fairly dangerous mood. He had an idea that this visit to Karsolithuyl was doomed to a violent conclusion and as he rested in the bath surrounded by his friends Zacxh felt the battle might as well begin sooner rather than later. The fact that he was naked and unarmed made little difference. He was in no mood to be patronized or even disturbed by this rude intruder.

Inarra gave away little of the circumstances surrounding the Spiderguard’s unannounced arrival in Karsolithuyl. She implied that they came on vague house business to pass through the area and that she and her friends only stopped due to the presence of Illithid in the area. She also made a point to mention that she and her companions had saved the lives and cargo of Karsolithuyl latest caravan which was led by Uuu. Inarra explained that he had promised save harbor by way of thanks.

At this Ttt became exceedingly cordial. She asked about the wellbeing of Uuu with whom she was obviously having an affair, and thanked the Spiderguard extravagantly (for a female) for his safe deliverance from the Mind Flayers.

The attitude of the meeting chanced in light of the caravan rescue and Ttt invited the Guard to stay as her guests. More specifically, she invited Sorn Nal and Valron (by far the most beautiful males of the guard) to join her 'anytime' for a 'private meeting.' No sooner had she finished speaking but Sorn leapt to his feet and indicated he was ready to depart for a 'meeting.' Naked and bathed in the harsh candlelight of the baths, it was obvious that he was indeed ready. Embarrassingly so.

The pair departed for many hours during which Inarra became more and more agitated. Apparently Sorns claims of bedding the gorgeous scout were more than mere boasts.

The following day, as Zacxh and the rest again basked in the opulent luxury of the Karsolithuyl baths, Ttt returned and assigned a guard to escort them to wherever they would care to tour. Apparently Sorn had made a very favorable impression. He was apparently quite skilled in *all* the forms of dance and Ttt made it clear that she looked forward to his extended stay.

Setting aside all the hanky panky and thoughts of rampant homicide, the actual reason for the Spiderguard's visit was to investigate the mystery of the tattoos found on insurgents in both Ossund and Orlylyl. The words on the tattoo matched too closely with the Karsolithuyl house motto to be coincidence. To that end Valron plied the bath attendants for information about tattoos and such, but he was either too obtuse, or the attendants were too astute or discreet. Valron learned nothing.

The guard which Ttt assigned, on the other hand, turned out to be a windfall of sorts. As Inarra plied him with her charms, Zacxh caught a glimpse of a tattoo which seemed to match those of the spies. A fiasco soon followed.

Inarra and Zacxh jumped into their distracting and infuriating routine. Zacxh informed the guide that he was rude to join them in a bathing chamber clothed and armed, while Inarra did a passable job trying to seduce him out of his clothes. She was pawing and rubbing him all over trying to better expose his incriminating tattoo when Ttt burst into the chamber. She was furious about something and began tossing around accusations of spying and taking advantage of her hospitality. She was entirely correct of course still, the change in her demeanor was pretty shocking. Especially so, thought Zacxh, because it occurred during a relaxing soak in the baths. He wondered how she could risen to the post of Captain and yet have so little regard for decorum.

Zacxh grudgingly hauled himself from his interrupted soak and began dressing. Inarra continued to verbally fence with the steward looking for some opportunity to learn more of the spies, or to at least cause more trouble. She scored another direct hit when she mentioned that she would enjoy meeting the steward to thank him or her personally for the hospitality. Ttt of course could not deny the request, but muttered something foreboding about such a meeting not to be attempted by the sane.

Spiderguard was led to the steward's chambers under heavy guard. She was not at all what Zacxh expected. Her demeanor was grandmotherly and her attitude distracted and impatient in a strange mixture which left one thinking that she was either completely inept or insane with underutilized power. Zacxh Liked her, thought on the Inept or insane issue, he assumed the latter.

She invited Spiderguard into her generous quarters, apparently afflicted with the addiction of all stewards... Tea. She passed around little cakes on a small platter and clapped her hands to begin the arduous ceremony.

The Spiderguard settled in and made small talk with the ancient elf. She was impatient to have them all settled and issued terse, grandmotherly commands.

Once everyone was settled and behaving like nice little murderers, the tea began. All sense of urgency dissipated as the very formal ritual began. Two stooped servants appeared from behind beautiful marble columns with servings concocted from a subtle blend if herbs and fungus. Zacxh looked to Sorn and Valron who seemed pleased with the aromatic stuff. They sipped in respectful silence of the ritual. Zacxh looked into the murky water in his cup and sniffed. He was not impressed and set the tea down untried. He appreciated the ceremony much more than the actual beverage and now that the formalized stooping and bowing was complete, had no real need for or interest in the few sips before him.

The steward began making small talk.

'It is interesting that the bag did not mention your coming. Usually it foretells of all visitors.'

This was the second reference to the mysterious 'bag.' This time there was no escaping the discussion. Inarra was clearly intrigued and seized the opportunity to find out more about this strange thing.

'Yes... Hmm... That does seem strange' She began. 'Well maybe 'the bag' did not know we were coming...

'Impossible, the bag sees and knows all.' The steward was incredulous at the suggestion.

Inarra, as always, stirred the pot a bit. 'Perhaps we could meet the bag and ask it.'

The steward froze in contemplation. She sat statue still a moment and the shrugged in resignation. 'Yes perhaps that is a good idea...'

Zacxh knew nothing of 'the bag' but was sure that 'meeting' it was probably going to be a bad idea.

The steward clapped her hands again and after only the briefest moment, a stooped servant appeared with a large silken pillow upon which lay a medium sized nondescript leather bag. The Spiderguard watched in rapt silence as the steward placed the bag into the center of her chamber and with her mouth hovering over the opening whispered a few phrases. She stepped back a pace or so and waited. The bag erupted.

The figure which emerged did so violently. If a genie pops out of a bottle in a cloud of smoke and a summoned hell hound steps from its native plane through a glowing portal then this thing arrived from the bag from whatever burning pit of hell it called home much as an elephant would emerge from the anus of a hummingbird. The arrival of the bag's inhabitant was one of the most unlikely and disturbing sights Zacxh could remember.

Ten feet of heavily muscled torso thrust forth stretching the now mucousy looking and elastic mouth of the bag well past its probable limits. It looked like a potato grub birthing an armless pig. The torso was topped by a bald head with cruel glowing eyes. The demons arms were grotesquely thin and under formed and tipped with fetid nails on long boney fingers. The demon’s mouth twisted into an annoyed scowl and the words which burst out in a powerful baritone shook the walls like an earthquake.

'WHY HAVE YOU AGAIN DISTURBED A B C D E F G!!!'

'That,' explained The steward in an almost bored tone 'is the Bag.'

The Spiderguard were all instantly on their feet reflexively stepping back a bit. This was definitely not what anyone had expected. Zacxh stood still as a stone and watched the demon with a professional eye. He wondered if the thing could be hurt and if he would be forced to find out. It was clearly a denizen of a darker and more sinister world and its presence here in Karsolithuyl seemed wrong on many levels.

Zacxh looked about to see the reaction of his companions. most had a shocked look to them and just starred, but Sorn was severely agitated, and looked like he was trying to disappear behind Trynn. Zacxh's suspicions were confirmed. The dancer definitely faded from view a bit and Zacxh caught a glimpse of stone wall right thought the little elf.

The demon glowered at the steward, then seemed to bristle a bit as if it had just heard an intriguing sound or noticed something familiar. It looked away from the steward and spun upon Sorn. It gazed directly at Sorn almost as if Trynn were not interposing the view at all.

It almost seemed to smile as it spoke.

'VENGANCE LITTLE ONE... YOU ONLY NEED ASK AND VENGANCE SHALL BE YOURS!'

Sorn cowered behind Trynn, but could not escape as the demon reached out its tiny arm. The arm was far to short to reach but the entire dimension of the room warped and constricted. The arm did not seem to stretch so much as the distance between the Demon and Sorn seemed to shrink. It touched Sorn’s shoulder and the two of them flowed like a pink, meaty vortex back into the tortured little bag. The Spiderguard was left gawking.

The steward was first to speak.

'Hmm. It has never done *that* before... Well, I guess that concludes tea, perhaps you all had better be on your way.'

Zacxh spoke up quickly. He hoped to set the tone for the verbal barrage which was sure to come from Inarra.

'Yes, well tea was lovely ma'am indeed, but perhaps we might impose upon your hospitality a bit longer to try and recover the elf your bag just... um... ate. He is after all an important member of our expedition.'

As expected, Inarra chimed in.

'Oh yes, our good friend Sorn cannot be left to this fate...'

Innara began her typical tactic of speaking of everything and nothing all the while watching the stewards face for some reaction to exploit. The Stewart seemed totally disinterested in Sorn's well being and was merely interested in the Bag's unusual behavior. Nonetheless Inarra convinced her to let the Spiderguard remain in her quarters while she tried a few things to sort the matter out.

The Stewart put her face back into the bag and began muttering for the demons return. She stayed at it much longer this time, and just when Zacxh was beginning to wonder if the Demon had somehow escaped its prison, the bag erupted again. This time it sent the steward sprawling back. Sorn stood at the 'feet' of the demon and looked a bit like he was enjoying himself. The demon spoke again.

'I SHALL DESTROY THE DESTROYERS. I SHALL AVENGE YOUR LOSS. JUST SAY THE WORD AND THE RECKONING SHALL BEGIN.'

Sorn looked as if he had consumed too much tea. His face was bright, his back straight. and his eyes seemed to have taken on a glow of their own much like those of the demon which hovered over the dancer like a protective parent. No one knew what to do and no one moved. Sorn looked apologetically toward Valron and uttered the command which he knew would spell doom.

'Valron, the Illithid amass outside the gates, we must be careful... '

He paused and looked about the room.

'Yes ABCDEFG, give me vengeance!'

The room went inky black with vile demonic energy. Somewhere a strange voice called out and the steward's guards began to die. Zacxh closed his eyes and reached out with his mind's eye to feel for the life force of those around him. He was suddenly acutely aware of all the hate and pain and fear around him. The room and every inhabitant therein appeared to his mind outlined by the ghosts of their repressed and most horrible memories. Zacxh drew his blade and cut off a guard who was groping forward trying to reach the steward. Zacxh felt his scars bristle and his arms swell with power as he hacked the guard down in the darkness.

The captain of the guard burst into the darkened room from her post just outside and was immediately attacked by the demon. The steward chanted some sort of spell and the Spiderguard for the most part drew back to the walls unsure of which threat to attack.

Zacxh ran past the Captain expertly slashing her from shoulder to shoulder. His blade cleaved through her armor but even blinded by the demon’s oppressive darkness, she twisted out of the way of most of the damage. She was a very skilled warrior, and got off with a fairly superficial wound. Sorn blasted the Captain with a bolt of his own dark energy which to Zacxh's mind eye appeared as a dark bolt of pure hate. The usually ineffective stuff slammed into the captain and drained much of her spiritual reserves. Sorn Grinned Evilly.

The Steward completed her incantation and the inky darkness which the demon had wrought constricted and coalesced into a dark ball in her upraised fist. She held the wad of hateful energy a moment before releasing it into a ray which slammed the demon mightily.

The demon roared in anger and reached out his tiny arm in that unsettling, space warping way, and slashed the steward across the chest with glowing claws. Where the nails found flesh, lines of blue demonic flame remained to burn on painfully. The steward seemed only vaguely aware of the attack and commented in an intrigued voice.

'Hmm it has never done that before either... how odd'

Zacxh attacked a nearby guard. He caught it off guard in all the confusion and nearly bisected the elf from neck to hip. Combat was joined all around between the Spiderguard, Sorn’s demon, and the steward with her guards. Zacxh charged the steward but was stopped in his tracks by the demon. The infernal beast's claws raked across Zacxh's chest, tore through the iron hardness of his scars as if they were not even there, and left three burning lines scraped into his skin. Pain and light erupted from the wound and Zacxh found himself wounded, and engulfed in demonic flame.

He called out enraged to Sorn.

'Hey Dancer, control that thing before it kills us all.'

Zacxh was suddenly acutely aware that his life was in serious danger. If those flames continued for too long they would overwhelm his minds ability to fend them off and begin devouring his flesh. He retreated into a corner, took up a defensive stance and began rummaging around his mind for a solution to his dilemma. He poured himself inward, but left enough sense behind to ward off attack. If that demon reached out for him again, it would loose at a finger. Zacxh did not want to feel those claws again.

His back against the wall, Zacxh ran the corridors of his mind looking for a means of fending off the flames. He did not have to look far and discovered a new trick.

The answer was, as always, provided by painful memories of his past. He focused his mind on the revulsion he felt about being consumed by flame and remembered a dozen occasions when he had been disfigured by the heat of brands and coals. His fears and revulsions, so central to his psyche, did the rest. Zacxh was vaguely aware that the battle had changed tempo and opened his eyes to survey the scene. The steward was dead and the demon gone. Bodies were strewn about the chamber and only the Spiderguard remained. Sorn looked a bit sheepish.

Zacxh looked to his burning chest and watched as his unconscious mind took over the fight. His skin closed over and the flames were pushed away from his body by the pure force of his revulsion to being burned. Unfortunately, the energy of the heat still built within Zacxh's mind. He needed a way to dissipate it quickly before it attacked his soul just like the flames had attacked his flesh. He knew what would happen, and knew his friends were not going to like it.

'Spiderguard! Close your eyes!!!' Zacxh already had his squeezed tight and was still seeing the world in shades of hate and anger through his mind's eye.

Things were about to get interesting. Now that they were tamed, those demonic flames were about to become his blazing ticket out of there. He only hoped that they would last long enough to do some good. Now it was Zacxh who was grinning.
