To edit a page, either double-click on the text in your browser, or click on the 'edit' link in your navigation bar on the right.

It will bring you to an editable text box that you can use to add, replace or delete text.

To learn more about the details of formatting, etc, go to the bottom of the front page of the UnderWiki; there's a section of links down there about wiki.
