Dark Charmer

Members of this prestige class are suffused with charm, allure, and presence. What the world does not see however, is the need to dominate, control and manipulate everything and everyone. And should those 'delicate' tactics fail, kill that which cannot be mastered, most often by the subtle use of poison. No sneak attacks in the dark for these folks. When you die it will be looking into their eyes, confused and surprised at the betrayal, and should the charmer be good enough, maybe even glad that their radiant face was the last thing you saw.

Based loosely on the Charmer prestige class http://www.hallofhero.com/PC/ViewPressClass.cfm?IDPC=61 and the Assassin Prestige class http://d20srd.org/srd/prestigeClasses/assassin.htm

Requirements

To qualify to become a dark charmer, a character must fulfill all the following criteria

Abilities

Charisma 18+

Skills

Diplomacy 8 Ranks, Intimidate 8 ranks

Spells/Powers

Ability to cast/manifest at least three spells from the school of enchantment/telepathy. At least one of these spells must be 3rd level or higher.

Special

The character must kill someone outside of melee using poison and the victim must know who did it.

Class Skills

Bluff (Cha), Concentration (Con), Craft (Int), Decipher Script (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Disguise (Cha), Forgery (Int), Gather Information (Cha), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (all skills, taken individually), Listen (Wis), Perform (Cha), Profession (Wis), Sense Motive (Wis), Sleight of Hand (Dex), and Spot (Wis). Psicraft* (Int) OR Spellcraft (Int) (as caster/manifester base class)

Class Features

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Charmers gain no additional proficiency in any weapon or armor.

Social Grace (Ex)

The Charmer gains a bonus to the skill diplomacy equal to his charmer level.

Poison Use

Dark Charmers are trained in the use of poison and never risk accidentally poisoning themselves when sliding a poisoned needle into someone's wrist, or swabbing contact poison across the back of a lovers neck (you get the point), oh yeah and putting it on blades too, etc.

Trustworthy (Ex)

As per the Trustworthy feat from Song and Silence (+2 bonus on all Diplomacy and Gather Information checks)

How Charming (Ex)

Beginning at 2nd level, the Dark Charmer becomes so charming, it becomes harder to resist his enchantment/telepathy spells/powers. The save DC for any enchantment/telepathy spell/power increases +1. This rises to +2 at 5th level and +3 at 8th level. These increases are cumulative with those granted by the Spell Focus and Greater Spell Focus feats, or their psionic equivalents.

Immune to Charms (Ex)

A Charmer of 2nd level or higher gains immunity to all charm spells and effects and powers. This does not include compulsions.

Arcane Grace (Ex)

The Charmer gains his Charisma bonus to all saving throws. 

Awe Inspiring (Ex) - deleting, replacing with Arcane Grace from 1st level

At 3rd level, a Charmer gains the ability to generate an intangible, invisible aura of awe that overwhelms hostile creatures within a 20-foot radius. Anyone about to attack the Charmer must attempt a Will save (DC = the Charmer's character level + charisma modifier). Those who fail the saving throw suffer a -2 morale penalty to attacks, checks, and save for one fully day or until they successfully damage the charmer generating the aura. A creature that made its initial save or damaged the charmer is immune to this aspect of the Charmer's aura for one day. Charmers ignore the aura of other charmers.

Persuasive (Ex)

As per the Persuasive feat from Song and Silence (+2 bonus on all Bluff checks and Intimidate checks)

Arcane Presence (Su)

The Charmer permanently gains two points to charisma.

Distracting Presence

Due to their alluring nature and charmed aspect, Dark Charmers gain their Charisma bonus to their AC. They retain this even when they would normally loose their Dexterity bonus (e.g. when flat footed). They loose this bonus if they are concealed from the attacker or behind cover. They also loose this bouns if they should wear armor heavier than light. 

 Fascinating (Sp) - deleting, replacing with Distracting Presence

The Charmer may cast mass suggestion once per day as a spell-like ability using his total class level to determine the caster level.

Gilded Tongue (Ex)

A Charmer of 7th level or above can speak with any living creature.

Alluring (Ex)

+2 bonus to Diplomacy and to the DCs of your mind-affecting language-dependant spells (requires Trustworthy, Persuasive and CHA 13+)

Speak the Truth (Su)

One of the Charmer's most powerful abilities: his or her aura so overwhelms others that they find it impossible to lie within the Charmer's presence. This effect is usable to a number of times per day equal to the Charmer's Charisma modifier. The effect has a duration of one minute per character level.

A True Charmer (Ex)

The Charmer uses a new table to determine NPC attitudes, which supersedes Table 5-4 in the DMG.

Other Feature ideas

Trying to think of something darker to replace the higher level class features

A Quick Escape - to replace Gilded Tongue?

Killing people up close so often necessitates an expeditious escape. The Dark Charmer may cast/manifest Dimension Door (psionic or magic as appropriate) 1/day as a free action. This is at their current caster/manifester level. (Similar to the Elocater 3rd level power, and the Master Diplomat prestige class)

Undetectable Lie - to replace Speak the Truth?

One of the Dark Charmer's most powerful abilities. His or her aura so overwhelms others that they find it impossible to detect when the Charmer is lying to them. This effect protects against such spells as Discern Lies, Zone of Truth, special abilities, and during such spells as Read Thoughts, Mind Probe and Probe Thoughts, where the charmer is forced to answer a question, they must answer but they can lie. This effect is usable to a number of times per day equal to the Charmer's Charisma modifier. The effect has a duration of one minute per character level. The Dark Charmer also recieves a +10 on Bluff checks opposed by Sense Motive checks (reverse of Inquisitor feat).

Precision poisoner - to replace True Charmer?

The Dark Charmer's poison use and precision placement reach a synergistic apex. Anytime the dark charmer uses poison against an individual outside of melee, scores a critical hit in melee or executes a coup de grace with a poisoned object, the target must make two savings throws for each damage phase. If either save fails the target suffers the poison's damage.

  • (From the Dark Blade prestige class in the Poisoncraft handbook)
