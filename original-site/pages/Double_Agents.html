Double Agents

The documents found in the mine led Captain Xulleyl to confirm that House Orlylyl appears to have been behind the invasions of the mines at Nerr'tenden. After Falt revealed Valron's identity as an Orlylyl noble, the party decided to attempt a double-agent role with Orlylyl.

Valron wrote a message to send to Valron's Mom, a High Priestess in Orlylyl. Here's what was sent: Valron's Message

The party was barely able to get to Erlynden for a secret meeting with Valron's Sister, where his mother showed up, listened to the full report of happenings in the mines, instructed Valron to 'get the mineral' and then gave a bag of diamonds to pay for the operations.
