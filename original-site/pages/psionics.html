psionics

Psionics are allowed in this game, although they are uncommon. It is important to note that we will be playing without 'transparency' between magic and psionics.

This means that psionic powers do not affect magic powers, and vice versa. For example, detect psionics will not reveal magic dweomers.

We will be using one portion of the much-hated first edition Psionics Handbook:

  • Each discipline requires its own prime requisite stat - psionicists are not all Int-based characters:

    • Clairsentience: Wisdom

    • Metacreativity: Strength

    • Psychokinesis: Intelligence

    • Psychometabolism: Constitution

    • Pschyportation: Dexterity

    • Telepathy: Charisma

    Psionic characters use the prime requisite stat for the purposes of determining bonus power points and DC of manifested powers.
