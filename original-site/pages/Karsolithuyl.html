Karsolithuyl

Karsolithuyl was until recently the seat of House Aleanana's power. Aleanana was pursuing several significant clandestine projects all of which were apparently disrupted coincidentally during the Spider Guard's visit to the outpost. During an audience with That Dude, Illithids raided Karsolithuyl and overcame its defense at every step. Surviving witnesses claimed that the Illithids were after information rather than simple feeding.
