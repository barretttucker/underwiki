House Orlylyl

[Orlylyl.gif]

House Orlylyl ('Guild of the Blade') is the 27th House. They are the only competitor for House Oussund in the sapphire trade.

Orlylyl's desperate and unlikely bid to gain control of Gaullidurth several decades ago has paid off handsomely; they are now a well-respected Middle House. After its war, Orlylyl chose to focus on fewer, highly-trained forces rather than large mediocre ones. Their soldiers are renowned fighters, and Orlylyl's name has popped up more than once in rumor as the likely hand behind some well-executed operations.

One of the handful of most valuable outposts, Gaullidurth is the Northern Branch gateway after Dusklyngh, and the sapphire mine there is the only one in competition with House Oussund's Nerr'tenden.

Valron has been revealed as an Orlylyl noble.

Increasing evidence supports the idea that Orlylyl was behind the incursion to Nerr'tenden's mines and that they have been disrupting caravans through the Catacombs, although the fact that Oussund caravans were the only ones untouched seems confusing.
