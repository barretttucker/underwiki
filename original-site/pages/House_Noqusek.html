House Noqusek

[Noqusek.gif]

House Noqusek ('Sacred to Adamantite') is the Seventh House of Dolthgurdar, holding a seat on the City Council.

Noqusek is renowned for their respect for the arts, particularly the performance arts, including music and dance. The House is also very pragmatic: they are well-defended by their soldiers, and have a powerful Cadre and clergy.

They own three outposts, two of significance: Llurth Dreier, a fresh mussel plantation and Dallnothax, a well-protected silver mine. These two outposts are only a few hours apart with no other outposts between them, providing significant assistance in times of trouble and also saving some toll fees on their mussels. Their third outpost is a small lumber farm.
