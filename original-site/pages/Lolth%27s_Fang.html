Lolth's Fang

The dark elf monk tradition is unique and sinister. Their philosophy emphasizes personal discipline of mind and body. In fact, they have a Nietzschean super-man view of themselves and are even sometimes arrogant and disdainful towards non-monks, seen as weak and undisciplined.

There is no official order, they have no monasteries, they have no leadership or hierarchy.

Their philosophy of mind/body discipline is reflected in their unique fighting style, and their dedication to personal advancement is embodied by their practice of culling weak members who are not capable of advancement.

There is a reason they are called Lolth's Fang.

They take special pride in developing their own unique monk feats.

They have an unofficial code.
