Drezen

Drezen is a fungus. It is the primary ingredient in the poison that is commonly used to incapacitate or render unconcious enemies of the dark elves. It's cultivation is generally a house endeavor, as the 'resources' needed to cultivate it seem to be in short supply (though specifics seldom leave the house), this makes Drezen a moderately precious commodity.

Dark elves are immune to the effects of the Drezen fungus.
