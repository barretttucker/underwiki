House Baenep

[Baenep.gif]

House Baenep ('Blessed by the Underdark') is the Second House of Dolthgurdar. As House Godendar is the undisputed First House, there are none below Baenep that can think of taking its #2 council seat.

House Baenep has developed a reputation for itself over the millennia as a strong patron of elven culture, supporting artists and the largest Sorceror's Cadre in the region. They maintain an efficient and professional army nearly twice the size of Godendar's, and have promoted more priestesses through the ranks than any other family.

For'tenden, a strategic access post and source of rare silkworms, is their most precious outpost. Other possessions include Fraxoleth, a mussels farm, and other minor outposts bringing water and food moss to market. Their outposts are nearly all concentrated in the Southern Branch.
