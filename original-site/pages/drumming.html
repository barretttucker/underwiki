drumming

Darkelf percussion is a marvel to hear; their acute hearing combined with darkelf precision and agility provide a vast tapestry of drumming and percussion music. From portable instruments all the way up to the large tahko drums, the darkelves love the rhythmic power and complexity of drumming. Drums are most often used as the musical accompaniment to dancing, and the audiences will sometimes get involved, adding clapping, slapping or whistling to the performance.
