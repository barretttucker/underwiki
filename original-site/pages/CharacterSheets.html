CharacterSheets

Character Sheet Resources

There are a ton of character sheets out there, including user-fillable PDF forms. Some even perform calculations. Here are some of my favorites:

Excel Based

The Hero Forge and Spell Forge sheets on the Yahoo SpiderGuard Groups page are huge and cumbersome but they yield good results. (I've figured out how to modify them to make a gestalt rules character, and I'm happy to help out with that for your character. -Eric)

Fillable PDF

You need the Professional version of Adobe Acrobat to save filled-out PDF forms, but anyone with Acrobat Reader can fill one out and print it.

  • The Hypertext d20 SRD site has a pretty modern looking PDF character sheet that performs calculations where possible. They also have an already filled-out example.

  • The Mad Irishman has one to check out.

Plain Old PDF

  • Wizards of the Coast has a number of character sheets available, with more than a few for specific character classes.

  • The Hypertext d20 SRD site also offers a plain-Jane, non-fillable PDF version.
