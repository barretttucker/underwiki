Rauvdril

The name Rauvdril means little to most dark elves. They believe that he is nothing but a character in imp's tales, a pretender to Lolth's greatness, exposed for a fraud and destroyed ages ago.

Now that Valron has revealed himself to be a priest of Rauvdril, the party is learning a bit more about the god.

Rauvdril is indeed a god, although one not often worshiped by dark elves. His domains of influence include Earth, Strength and Travel.

There are Ten Virtues that his followers must live by - to follow these codes is to live morally, to fail at any of them is to be weak.

Rauvdril’s Ten Virtues

Courage – be brave, be strong, do not show fear

Prowess – be excellent, be admired, be skilled, especially on the battlefield

Exploration – find new paths, new places, new things

Self-Reliance – don’t expect others to do your work for you

Discipline – stay the course, show inner strength, focus on the goal

Self-Improvement – always strengthen yourself to strengthen Rauvdril

Exert your Will – shape your environment, don’t let it shape you

Force is a Last Resort – save your strength for the times it is truly needed

Act with Determination – when the time comes to act, be swift and strong

Inspire Loyalty – impress and draw others to you by living these virtues
