Catacomb Bandits

Band of toughs hiding out in the Catacombs between Bhaerynden and Llurth Dreier responsible for attacking several caravans bound for Dolthgurdar. According to Sharb, they were conscripted by Flikk under orders from House Orlylyl. Bhaerynden gold recovered from their hideout was melted down and used to finance their employment with House Oussund in Nerr'tenden after their defeat by the Spider Guard and recruitment by Nal. The circumstances of their loyalty to Nal remain a mystery. The Spider Guard assisted the bandits in making it easy for Orlylyl to believe they had been completely routed by ransacking the bandits' hideout but leaving a stash of House Godendar rubies for their former masters to find.
