Nerr'tenden

Nerr’tenden is a relatively isolated roughside stronghold about 100 miles (a week of hard travel) from the city of Dolthgurdar, where House Oussund is headquartered. (See the regional map.) The stronghold protects a small spring of clear water (which happens to be secretly collocated with one of the two known sources of Hirthal) and serves as a base of operations for mining gems, specifically sapphires. Nerr'tenden is one of more than 50 mining or farming outposts that all support the marketplace at Dolthgurdar.

There are roughly 70 dark elves in residence at Nerr’tenden at any one time, and 80-100 slaves. Approximately 60-80 of the slaves are mining laborers and haulers; their numbers vary with mortality rates and on the speed of replenishments from Dolthgurdar. The laborers are mostly duergar, with a handful of orogs in the mix. The remainder are expert miners, duergar slaves who plan and direct the efforts of the laborers. The remaining 20 slaves at Nerr’tenden are split evenly between gemcutters and household servant-slaves; the gemcutters are svirfneblin and a duergar; the household servants are dark elves who are indentured or slaves.

The inhabitants of Nerr’tenden are a mix of blacksmiths, fletchers, cooks, guards and the like.
