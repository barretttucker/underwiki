Geldiirn

Geldiirn'Olg Oussund is the Steward and Sword Master of Nerr'tenden. His skill with blades, loyalty and ambitions have earned him the rare honor of the House Oussund name.

As Steward, Geldiirn manages the daily affairs of Nerr’tenden with competence and charm. He is good at what he does; and the fact that he handles the headaches of administration is not lost on Dinlin. Out in the remote stronghold, she lets him manage with more of a free hand than most males have.

He relies on Xulleyl to coordinate guard activities to protect the hold, guard the caravans and keep watch over the slaves. She keeps the guard units on careful rotation, and it is her ambitions that have caused her to create the Spider Guard for her own uses.
