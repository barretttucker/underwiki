House Noquiryn

[Noquiryn.gif]

House Noquiryn ('Sacred to History') is the 14th house.

Noquiryn is a typical middle-powered house; they don't have the same across-the-board strengths and cultural achievements of Council Houses, as much of their wealth is dedicated to training what Sorcerors and Priestesses they have.

Their strategically located outposts, Cloibbrath and Erlynden catch toll traffic from every outpost on the outer West Branch. These outposts are the key to Noquiryn power.
