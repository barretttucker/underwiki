The Mineral

Nickname for mysterious destinations marked on maps recovered from (presumably) House Orlylyl operatives in the Nerr'tenden mines. The "Mineral" chambers were recently opened by the Zaxch and found to contain overwhelming magical auras and strange caustic liquid in ceramic flasks. At least one of the two uncovered chambers binds a large number of demons. Sorn summoned The Demon with the name that will never be remembered who said that his kin had been sealed there by Illithids. It seems that the illithid incursions into the Dolthgurdar region may be intended to protect the integrity if not the secret of the chambers, and that House Aleanana may be pulling the strings of the conflicts in which the Spider Guard has become embroiled.
