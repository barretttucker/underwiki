poison

Poisons are a common weapon in the dark elf arsenal. From common poisons like 'sleep juice' (used in ambushes, served on crossbow quarrels) to horrifically lethal or crippling toxins appropriate for ending a bloody vendetta, poisons are a weapon of choice.
