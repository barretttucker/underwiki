Episodes

The Invitation note that announced the beginning of the campaign.

Very brief summaries of the Spider Guard's adventures so far:

0. Homeward Bound in which the characters travel from Dolthgurdar to Nerr'tenden.

1. First Blood in which the characters learn about each other's abilities and have a leader selected.

2. Mines Incursion in which the characters defeat a large covert invasion of the mines at Nerr'tenden.

3. The Stoneshapers in which Stoneshaper mages visit Nerr'tenden, and one of them turns out to be an agent.

4. Illiam's Caravan in which the party brings Illiam and Keniryn gold to Llurth Dreier, dead or alive.

5. Orlylyl Family in which Valron's mother orders the Spider Guard to complete the task of the failed Miners Incursion.

For more detailed narrative from Zacxh's perspective, see ZacxhBlog.
