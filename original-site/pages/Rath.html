Rath

Female Tunnelrunner of House Kilep. Hard nosed leader and pragmatic, chose to trust the PCs when she needed to. Was the only survivor of the escort party for Valron, Zacxh, and Nal.

Presumed executed by Lt. Ardul or his agent upon her arrival in Nerr'tenden to contain information about the Spider Guard, she was actually employed by Captain Xulleyl as a scout and operative.
