tea

Tea is an important social ritual for Darkelves. It is an important part of ceremony and formality, but among close peers it is a relaxing and comfortable way to share time and companionship. Every community has a tea master and a tea house, however informal or basic.

Teas are made of various mosses, lichens and mushrooms found in the tunnels, waterways and chambers of the earth.

poisons are common among darkelves, but it would be an incredible affront to the darkelf society to murder or incapacitate an enemy with tea. When enemies truly cannot trust their hosts, they may take tea of the norzzen moss.
