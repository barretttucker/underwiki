destiny

Dark Elves sometimes refer to themselves as 'the Chosen'. They truly believe that their special powers and triumphant history are an indication of some divine favor, or at least a sign of primacy in the global food chain. Despite this, Dark Elves often spend an inordinate amount of time (in the perspective of shorter-lived races) planning, re-planning and plotting to account for every last possibility. They take nothing for granted.
