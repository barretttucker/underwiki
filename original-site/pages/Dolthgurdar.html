Dolthgurdar

Dolthgurdar is the regional capital and marketplace for a region roughly 100 miles across (as the umber hulk burrows). It is home to roughly fifty noble houses, and is headquarters for all of them. The city is managed by the City Council, whose members adjudicate disputes and make policy decisions.

Its three enormous gates are open for a steady stream of trade that comes in from the entire region.

The First House of the city, House Godendar, guards the city gates and oversees the enormous complexity of trade caravans, markets and disputes that come along with such an active marketplace.

House Oussund is the 28th House of the city.
