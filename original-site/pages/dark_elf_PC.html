dark elf PC

As with characters of every other race, the variety among Dark Elf characters and personae is limited only by the imagination of the player. What follows are some notes that should help you to understand some core Dark Elf traits as you design and think through the persona of your PC; I hope to add some depth to your perspective of this fascinating race, and change some misconceptions along the way. These should not be treated as absolutes - they are merely representative of the race as a whole.
