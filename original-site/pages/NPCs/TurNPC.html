NPCs/TurNPC

Tur is... was... a female tunnelrunner in charge of the caravan that brought Trynn to Nerr'Tenden

Cruelly murdered by Xulleyl's men at the gates of Nerr'Tenden along with her slaves and retainers, presumably to ensure secrecy.

Two of her retainers, Hurm and Drak, were small-time toughs who attempted to mug Trynn not once but twice during the trip. Trynn managed to talk his way out of it once but was finally forced into using violence to dissuade them. Acting quickly, knowing he was flanked and outnumbered, Trynn dispatched Drak down a deep hole before dealing with Hurm by knocking him unconscious.

When Tur confronted them and discovered Hurm's plan to mug and/or eliminate Trynn, she threatened to leave him alone in the underdark unarmed, but relented.

Later, when the caravan was slaughtered, Trynn noticed Hurm lying dead, his final act an attempt to shield Tur's body from the assassins' arrows.
