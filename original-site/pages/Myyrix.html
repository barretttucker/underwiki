Myyrix

Appearance

Of average height for a male, though more squarely built than the average Drow, Myyrix wears a chain shirt, well-worn leather breeches and shirt, and carries a matched set of long- and short-swords. He bears the scars expected from one working amongst the house guard.

His manner seems almost distracted at times and given to tangential comments, though few believe him absent minded after working with him for even a short time.
