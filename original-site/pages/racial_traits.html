racial traits

These are modified from the Forgotten Realms Underdark book.

Stat Adjustments:

  • +2 Dexterity

  • -2 Constitution

  • +2 Intelligence

  • +2 Charisma

Immunities:

  • sleep spells and effects

Racial Bonuses:

  • +2 v. enchantment spells and effects

  • +2 on Will saves v. spells and spell-like abilities

  • +2 bonus to Listen, Search and Spot checks

  • passing within 5 feet of a concealed or secret door entitles the dark elf to a Search check to notice it as if they were actively looking for it

Vision:

  • a dark elf has darksight out to 120 feet

  • a dark elf suffers from light blindness

Spell Resistance:

  • 11+ character level

Spell-Like Abilities: A dark elf with Intelligence of 13 or higher can use the following spell-like abilities once per day (caster level = character level)

  • dancing lights

  • darkness

  • faerie fire

Weapon Proficiencies: A dark elf receives the following as bonus feats:

  • Martial Weapon Proficiency (rapier)

  • Martial Weapon Proficiency (short sword)

  • Exotic Weapon Proficiency (hand crossbow)

Automatic Languages:

  • Undercommon

  • Dark Elf
