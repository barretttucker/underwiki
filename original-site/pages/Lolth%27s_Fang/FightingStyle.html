Lolth's Fang/FightingStyle

The Fang seek to dominate their opponents through choke holds, pressure points, stun moves, and trips. Anything to keep a foe under control. While they aren't well suited to front-line combat, they make excellent assassins, bodyguards, and bounty hunters.

Dark elf monks usually prefer to deal non-lethal damage. The monk "culls" are always fought using non-lethal damage.
