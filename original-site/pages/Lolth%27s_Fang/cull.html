Lolth's Fang/cull

The Fang have a particularly ruthless tradition called the "cull". If you are crippled, elderly, or otherwise too weak to continue advancing in your skills, a monk will challenge you to a duel. The duel is a farce of course, since you are obviously too weak to fight effectively. The shame of being culled is great, and this keeps the monks always training rigorously to avoid such a fate.

The culls are always fought with non-lethal damage for a knockout. The culled monk is now an outcast to the monks. She will never find another trainer to further her skills, and the Fang will disown her. She is dead to them.

While this is a decentralized, ad-hoc enforcement mechanism ripe for abuse, it is rarely abused because the only thing more shameful than being culled is to be beaten by the one you are attempting to cull. If this happens the loser usually accepts total banishment. Some monks with failed culls have gone to the surface the shame is so great. So it is a self-enforcing tradition and works well.

Culls generally only occur between monks of the same skill level as it is considered shameful to cull someone with obviously less experience than you. Although disguise *is* a typical monk skill, and dark elves *are* devious. Also, goading your enemy into culling you by feigning weakness would not be unheard of...
