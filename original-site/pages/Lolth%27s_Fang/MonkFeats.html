Lolth's Fang/MonkFeats

Dark elf monks value self-training and indepedence. They take special pride in developing their own unique fighting moves. Like wizards with custom spells, unique feats created by a monk are guarded closely, only passed on to trusted companions or apprentices. Even the rumour of having created a devastating new move can bring much prestige to a monk.

It is said that the monk who developed the "Blinding Fist" special strike had it given to him as a gift from Lolth for some wicked service he completed for her ...

There are rumors of particularly devastating feats with effects so wondrous they can only be supernaturally inspired. Only the most powerful monks and those who hire them know if the rumours are true. One such legendary move is often referred to as "I'll knock you into the middle of next week". Apparently, it can actually knock you into the middle of next week.
