Lolth's Fang/reason

The monks are often employed by the clerics as bodyguards, bounty hunters, and assassins.

This is so well-known that it is rumoured that the monks are a secret army for the clerics, or even Lolth herself. Because of this they are often referred to "Lolth's Fang" or simply "the Fang".

Some even fear that the monks may be the unwitting tools of a powerful outsider preparing an army for a sinister revolution. The monks scoff at this but sometimes even they begin to wonder where some of the darker knowledge in their special moves has come from.

The clerics are too arrogant and self-satisfied to fear anyone, much less a bunch of obsessive meat pounders like the monks, so they use the monks as tools, like they do everyone else. The Fang have no special loyalty to each other and as often as not they find themselves enemies in the employ of two different House clerics.
