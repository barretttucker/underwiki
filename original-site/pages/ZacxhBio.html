ZacxhBio

“Crack!... Crack! Crack!”

Lucinda’s vicious snake headed whip bit again and again.

“Crack”

“You ungrateful little bastard! Don’t you even have the sense to cry out. You would deprive your mistress of the pleasure of your song!!! Well, I will see to it that you sing.”

“Crack!”

Zacxh knew his reactions or lack thereof would have little effect on the outcome of this beating. He had been whipped too many times before to be conned by the enraged words of a priestess. She would whip him until either he fell insensible or until she found something else to appease her cruelty. In Lucinda’s case it would probably be the former. She loved to see a male fall victim to her wrath.

“Crack, Crack, Crack…” “Scream you insolent bastard!!!”

A few years ago he might have even done it. He might have thought that by screaming he would appease her in some way. It would work on mistress Serinill, maybe on Mistress Theria too. But this Lucinda, well she was a real piece of work. His only defense would be his inevitable weakness.

Therein lay the problem. His weakness was beginning to fail him. His body had grown large and tough it is true, but that was not it. Larger more formidable males would have collapsed by now. Zacxh had found a ‘hiding spot’ in his mind. It was strange, and in a way he knew it had been there all along, but now he could run there and take shelter. He used to feel a great sense of despair and loss when his body was beaten and deformed, and it was just that attachment that had concealed the hiding spot from him. He now knew that his body was not related to his mind, but was simply its vehicle.

Today His vehicle was going to be beaten into a bloody rag.

“Crack” “Fall you insolent pig”

Zacxh could hear the Snake headed whip. He could, in a detached way, feel the painful neurotoxin of the vipers every bite. But the pain lived in his nerves and his brain. It no longer penetrated his mind. Unfortunately, that meant that his battered, branded, tattooed, and pierced frame would have to fend for itself. Loss of blood and shock would eventually bring it down, but it would take much longer. This was not pleasing to the Mistress.

Actually she had found other more satisfying subjects for her pleasure, and seemed almost apathetic and mechanical in her abuse these days.

“Crack!” “I will flay your flesh and feed it to the worgs you insolent pig, now cry out your devotion to me or DIE!!!”

Zacxh smiled instead. He found it sad and slightly amusing that she was so disinterested in his torture that she had called him ‘pig” again. She had definitely lost interest.

It wasn’t always this way. For a time, he had actually even been favored by the priestess of House Oussund. They had utterly destroyed his family in a raid thereby raising the status of their house. He was so young then. Barely even a boy. He had been born into the role of Guard-leader aspirant. Had his house survived, he would have an entire section under his command even now. He would have been killing priestesses in routine raids and watching *them* cry out for mercy. Ah well. His lot was not so bad.

He had recently come to appreciate his position. He was a pit fighter, and a champion of House Oussund. As such, great care was taken to ensure he was as prepared as possible for his next bout. As a child he had learned to pounce on other boys and orcs and lizard folk quickly and quietly. He knew just where to slide his knife to still a beating heart or disable a troublesome sword arm. He had killed off all his kin in the first year or so of his enslavement. He was strong cunning and quick in combat and thus proved to House Oussund his worth.

Like any prize they kept him in good repair, or at least fully functional. His missing digits were painfully and grudgingly replaced by chiding priestesses who beat him senseless with clubs and whips for the favor. His right ear was a tattered rag and his ebony skin was so stitched with grey scars that it was difficult in some areas to find any virgin flesh at all. His knuckles were thick and knobby from repeated breaking and his nose was flat and round like an orc’s. His tattoos and marks of ownership were all but indiscernible anymore, lost in the tattered menagerie of his flesh

Yes, She would beat him into a pulpy pile of goop. Then he would be salved and stitched up by some servant and left to his fevers and dementias. He would lie in a painful ball for days or weeks and would eventually come to his senses. Then a bout. Would it be a beast again? Maybe a young troll. He would be given a spear or sword, sometimes a net or sharpened stick. He would be cast into darkness or blindingly painful light and shoved into some cramped space to fight to the death. Then, as a reward for victory he would be forced to couple with some priestess or another who would chastise and goad him for his lack of enthusiasm then proceed to beat him again into a bleeding retching pile of goo. It was an endless cycle and would eventually end in death. Zacxh had grown accustomed to the cycle and had actually begun to look forward to his fights, but now he knew he was in danger. His lack of care for his body’s wellbeing was beginning to become apparent and so he no longer was of use to the priestesses of Loth. He would soon be sold to the mines or tortured to death or sacrificed in some way. He knew his end was near. The most likely form of his demise would be some skilled of overpowering adversary in the pit. His priestesses would drug or disable him in some way and bet heavily against his victory.

Perhaps today would be that day. He had heard whisperings that he was to be wrapped in leather armor and masked to disguise his wounds and sent into the pit against a renowned champion of another house. If true, He was doomed. His arms and legs were hanging limp and near lifeless. Even the dangerous venom of a green mobeus spider would barely stimulate him enough to walk. He would clutch his spear and drag himself about, but there would be no strength. Yes, at long last, this was probably going to be the day.

Zacxh had barely noticed, but the beating had stopped. Where had everyone gone? He pulled himself up and found that the door to the arena was not open. He would not fight today. Instead, the passage to the observing deck lay open. He was meant to watch a bout? He struggled with the idea a moment as he had never watched one before, but soon crept up the stairs to a private observation booth. No one would see him here and there was a not addressed to him on the seat.

Two combatants entered small private arena. All around were other observation booths shrouded in darkness. How many Dark Elves were watching. One of the combatants was a large and only slightly drugged ogre the other had the same build and stance as Zacxh. Both warriors were armed with small sharp pikes and maked to hide their faces. The elf was scarred and torn of body and watching him was like looking in a reflecting pool.

The combat was short. The Dark elf died on the ogre’s pike after only a single failed parry. Zacxh opened the note.
