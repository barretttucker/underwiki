dark elf

The Dark Elf race is a subset of elf that lives in the caverns and tunnels of the earth. Dark Elves refer to themselves as 'Dark Elves' or 'The Chosen'; they do not use the burn epithet of 'Drow'. They are a highly organized society that is equal parts cooperation and competition. Their inherent superiority above other races has allowed them to thrive and grow at the expense of lesser beings. Naturally, this development has encouraged lesser beings to fear and hate their betters. Read more about the Dark Elf moral code.

Dark Elves live for a very long time, as do their Burn cousins. Since Dark Elves don't marry and often change partners over the course of the centuries (and the fact that males die more frequently in the normal day-to-day of Dark Elf politics), they trace their lineage through the maternal side. Over millennia, this has led to a resource preference for the females - they are significantly stronger and larger than the males, and they hold the positions of social power and authority among their communities (like that of the Matriarch).

Physically, Dark Elves are beautiful, slim people who stand 4 1/2 to 5 feet tall, with only the tallest females approaching 5 1/2 feet in height. Weight is correspondingly slight and ranges from 75 to 120 pounds. Their skin ranges from dark grey to obsidian black, with hair of white or colored hue. Dark Elves have beautiful eyes of bright colored irises surrounding enormous pupils.

Dark Elves are fully adapted to their unlit and harsh environment, with darksight, innate resistance to spells and an uncanny agility of body and mind that allows them to easily navigate the twisting rise and fall of tunnels and chimneys deep in the earth. See dark elf racial traits.

Like their Burn cousins, Dark Elves are very aesthetically sophisticated, and dedicate a significant amount of time and energy into the Arts, crafts, and performance.
