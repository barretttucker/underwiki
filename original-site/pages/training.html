training

Training rules have been disabled.

The Concept:

In this campaign, you will need to spend training time under the tutelage of a master (who you must find and hire) in order to advance a level. In the absence of a master, you will need to spend time teaching yourself and working on bettering your techniques in solitude. You are not required to drop what you are doing and begin training once you earn enough xp to level, but don't get the benefits of a new level until you have completed the training for it.

All training time (with or without a master) can be spent consecutively or broken up. Note that you continue to accrue experience points at all times; xp earned while waiting for training is not lost. You could theoretically go up more than one level in a row, but must spend the time and master fees for each level attained.

The Training Process:

  1. Consult the appropriate table below for your base training time required to level up.

  2. If self-training, make Training Rolls until you succeed.

  3. Repeat for each 'class' if gestalt, if standard, you're done!

Training Rolls:

When self-training, you must make a Training Roll: an ability check of DC = 10 + target level; add the bonus for your prime requisite for the class in question. For example, you are self-training to level 4 as a fighter, so make a strength check of DC 14 at the end of the training period.

Failed checks can be repeated each week with a cumulative +2 bonus to the check for each failed check while training a class to a new level. The DC doesn't change; the +2 bonus is added to your roll.
