competition

Competition among darkelves is of absolutely paramount importance for individuals and the House they serve. Every darkelf house wishes to be on the City Council; if it's already there, it wishes to be higher in prominence. While the moral code stresses darkelf cooperation for survival of the race, the Rules also specifically demand the destruction of weakness, regardless of the cost.

Each individual darkelf also strives to out-maneuver peers and competitors for the choicest positions, favors and access to other leaders and opportunities. The desire for both individual and House primacy leads to intense underhanded dealings and vicious battles across every aspect of darkelf resources, from trade routes and social position to subterfuge and assassination. Houses have even been known to destroy other Houses entirely - if one House can kill all living members of a Family without witnesses from other noble families being brought to the City Council, it will go unpunished. In fact it will rise in the rankings, assuming the family was above it (and why would a House spend such resources to eliminate a weaker one?).

Naturally, darkelves go to great lengths to avoid being caught in any activities that would overtly violate the Code.
