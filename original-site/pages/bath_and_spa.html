bath and spa

The bath is a standard comfort of darkelf society. Waters are heated or drawn from a natural hot spring, scented with herbs and used for soaking tired and tense darkelf muscles. Skilled bath masters prescribe herbal mixtures and compounds for particular maladies, provide massage and tend to guests in the bath house.
