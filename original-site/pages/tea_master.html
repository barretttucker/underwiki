tea master

An official Tea Master has spent some years to become certified in their trade. The Tea Master's responsibilities are critical in providing a sophisticated and civilized tea to House seniors and to outsiders. In smaller places, like Nerr'tenden, it would be more usual for a House to assign an apprentice or even a hobbyist to this role; Tea Masters can be quite costly.
