Cleric

PC's can't be clerics in this campaign, as this is a highly restrictive class to play. Only females can be clerics, and only clerics of Lolth are sanctioned in this society.

Females who do become clerics of the Spidery Queen aren't good matches for gaming because they are extremely restricted in their activities - they spend the vast majority of their time either governing and making policy decisions or keeping non-clerics in line with proper respect for Her.

Clerics pretty much have their time cut out for them and that's the way it is.
