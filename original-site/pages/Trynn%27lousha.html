Trynn'lousha

Trynn is large and strong for a dark elf. He keeps to himself and stares too long when making eye contact.

He moves gracefully, like a trained dancer. He wears no armor, and carries a whip, a flail, and a net. Dark elves easily recognize these as the favorite weapons of the dark elf monk tradition, known commonly as Lolth's Fang.

Trynn's back and arms and legs show many scars, clearly inflicted by scourge and whip. These are the sure marks of a cleric's slave.
