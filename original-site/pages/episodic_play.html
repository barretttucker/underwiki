episodic play

Many games play in live, continuous action. This game will feature "episodes" that may be followed by breaks in the timeline. These breaks could last for hours, days, weeks, or even years. As the PC's complete (successfully or not) various episodes, there will likely be downtime afterwards.

The downtime can be used for a variety of things - training to a new level, traveling to a new place, resuming usual activities, or even individual actions, correspondence or adventures.
