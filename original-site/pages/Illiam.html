Illiam

Understandably paranoid as a member of the small House Keniryn family, he is known for the small fortune represented by his personal armor and ornaments as well as for his skill as a dancer.

Steward Illiam Kenyrin was mysteriously killed while escorting a caravan of Bhaerynden gold to Dolthgurdar under the protection of his outpost's most elite warriors as well as that of the Spider Guard during an ambush by bandits who had been harrying caravans in the catacombs between Bhaerynden and Llurth Dreier. His body was conducted by the Spider Guard the rest of the journey through the catacombs as none of the Kenyrin contingent survived the ambush.

Taken to Dolthgurdar by Kenyrin forces, Illiam was ressurected by his family and has now resumed his duties in Bhaerynden.

It has been rumored that Illiam has some secret liaison with Captain Xulleyl of Nerr'tenden.
