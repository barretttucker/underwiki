station

Dark Elf dealings among their own kind are often tinged with arrogance and egotism; other races see nothing but arrogance, dismissal and indifference from these powerful people. When dealing with those who are more powerful, however, Dark Elves are absolutely ready to show humility. It is a sign of respect to your betters, and increases the likelihood that they will support you in exchange for some services or relationship. Dark Elves don't live long without being sensitive to their place and station compared to those around them.
