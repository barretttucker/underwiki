House Godendar

[Godendar.gif]

House Godendar (the 'Dark Hearts') is the ancient First House of Dolthgurdar. As the preeminent house, they have significant responsibilities in the city and marketplace. As the keepers of the city, they take tax on all trade by all houses.

Godendar wields vast influence in the region; no house controls more resources or collects more tolls than Godendar. They are significant patrons of the arts, have a powerful stable of merchants, and only House Baenep has more soldiers, priestesses and sorcerors. Godendar's coffers are reputed to be worth more than the next eight richest houses combined.

In addition to its duties in Dolthgurdar, the House has several outlying possessions, including the distant carving-mushroom farms at Rhinn and the ruby mines at T'lethtyrr, an outpost very near to Nerr'tenden.
