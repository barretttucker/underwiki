Homeward Bound

The PC's travel from Dolthgurdar to Nerr'tenden as part of caravans. They travel as several groups or individuals, not knowing that the others have been summoned the same way.

Along the way, various troubles manifest themselves, but there does appear to be a troubling event common to all three: a ruby caravan from the House Godendar mines at T'lethtyrr has been ambushed and its rubies captured.

Each caravan ends up meeting an uncertain or grim fate at the end of the arduous journey, and the players are each brought to their quarters in the stronghold by Lt. Ardul.
