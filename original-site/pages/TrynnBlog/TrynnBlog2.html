Dear Diary,

A lot has happened to me since my miserable failure on my first assignment as a bodyguard for the house clerics.

My only hope is that even Shaille's death was part of the ruse but I fear that Xulleyl is far too ruthless to risk leaving her alive. Shaille is dead, it is my fault, and the Oussund priestesses will have my mother tortured to death for certain if it is ever discovered that I yet live. In fact, my failure alone may be enough that they are punishing her anyway, even as I write this. It is a daily struggle to stay put and not attempt an escape in order to return to Dolthurgar.

Since the attack and my fake death I have been sequestered to Nerr'Tenden as part of some mad plot orchestrated by this Xulleyl who claims to be a Captain of the Guard for House Oussund. I have my doubts about that.

Upon my arrival she ruthlessly exterminated the cargo caravan that brought me. I am certain this was done more for my benefit than for any value to be had in eliminating the caravan. She was sending a message, trying to establish fear and dominance before even meeting me.

Well it worked. The callous massacre chilled me to the bone and I have no desire to cross Xulleyl. I doubt I would survive the day if I even brought her an instant of displeasure. This is the only reason I do not attempt escape. I owe it to mother to be wise and survive so that I can still save her in the future. If I were to be caught in mid-escape it would be my death.

Only the future can tell where this will all lead.

Signing off, By Lolth's Webs
