Clues

This is a place for stray bits of knowledge and clues that you come across.

From Zacxh:

The old mining foreman was very trusted by the miners and knowledgable. She retired, and was replaced by an incompetent and paranoid buffoon *just* before the cave ins. (the new foreman is a cousin of Lt. Ardul)

The poison in the shipment was not suited to attacking a water supply.

The forecasted tunnels on the infiltrator maps lead toward the eight pointed symbol. None reach them yet.

Could it be related that a large shipment of rubies recently went missing?

The first contingent of gaurds that Valron and Zacxh encountered on their first foray into the mines (one day before the main party) wore an alternating mix of house Oussund uniforms and stealthy rogue type outfits.

The body with no blood and a happy look on its face had no visible wounds, (or bite marks that Zacxh or Valron could find) and was discovered just outside the cave-in near the secret entrance. He was not recognized by any of the 'regular' miners and was dressed in rogue wear with a cloth-silenced shortsword.

There was a spy who Zacxh and Valron brought to Captain Xulleyl. The spy was named Jhul (Like 'Jewell'). Captain X asked Zacxh to get him quickly and bring him to her. We abducted him from the chowhall and dragged him unconcious to the Captain's quarters. He was a member of Lt. Ardul's troop. (The Lt. was not pleased that we were screwing with his men without his permission). Captain X showed the spy a note (possibly the one from Sorn's pocket???) and when Jhul reacted in fear, Captain X decapitated him on the spot. We departed immediately to come find you (and kill a few dozen cave dwellers allong the way.)

Lt Ardul took the vast haul of recovered rubies from Zacxh, Valron, and Nal upon their arrival to Nertenden. It was later discovered that he did not report the treasure to The Captain. This occured several weeks before the mine incident. Coincidence???

Lt. Ardul seems central to many of the happenings in the mines.

His cousin was forman during the cave in.

His trooper was a spy (Jhull).

He took the rubies for himself(did not inform the Captain of them).

He has been forbidden to work with the spidergaurd.
