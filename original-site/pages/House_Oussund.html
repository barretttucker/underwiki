House Oussund

[Oussund.gif]

House Oussund ('Heirs to the Spider's Kiss') is the 28th House.

House Oussund is one of a handful of lower Middle Houses that are in good position to further themselves in the coming years. Oussund has a thriving commitment to training Sorcerors and Priestesses, and maintains a healthy military presence. Oussund has several notable artists in Dolthgurdar, including the dancer Tal'nalthereen Oussund.

The very remote sapphire mining outpost called Nerr'tenden is the family's source of income.

The House's current rivals include House Orlylyl, which is the only competitor in the sapphire market, and House Keniryn, which controls a gold mine and armed checkpoint only a half-day of travel from Nerr'tenden. House Oussund caravans must pass through the gold mine checkpoint to reach the market at Dolthgurdar, and tolls are expensive.
