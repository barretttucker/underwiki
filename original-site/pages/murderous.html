murderous

Dark Elves are often assumed to be on the lookout for gleeful killing sprees at all times. This is far from the truth. A Dark Elf would not waste their time with killing unless it gained something specifically desired - killing is a lot of work and requires a lot of thought:

In order for a killing to be useful, many details need to be thought through: should the body be found or not? If so, when and by whom and for what purpose and under what circumstances? If not, why not, and should the fact be discovered later or not, etc? Regardless of these, what are the next actions that should be taken?
