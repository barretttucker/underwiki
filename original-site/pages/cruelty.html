cruelty

Most players believe that Dark Elves have an insatiable hunger for death, blood and cruel treatment of others. This is incorrect. Dark Elves have the capacity for cruelty, as every other race does. However, why should a Dark Elf waste time or resources with cruelty, torture, etc?
