marketplace

The Dolthgurdar marketplace is a dynamic and powerful confluence of goods, money and ambition. Several dozen commodity goods and crafts are traded daily at the marketplace. Caravans from the mining and farming outposts bring in enough trade to support the region's population of nearly 115,000 full-time residents and slaves.

The First House guards the trade goods and oversees trade at the marketplace.
