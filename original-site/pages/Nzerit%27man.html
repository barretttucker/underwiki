Nzerit'man

Nzerit'man translates literally to Nzerit[in search of]'man[weakness].

It is generally manifested as a house-sponsored 'cleansing' of elements within the house that are considered or evaluated in the eyes of the Nzerman [Seeker of weakness] to be weak and unworthy of support by the house. A 'cleansing' generally results in death.
