Zilsh

Zilsh was the scout and assistant for Rath, who led a tunnel runner caravan for House Melemtor. He was friendly and cheerful with the PC's he met in the caravan (Nal, Zacxh and Valron).

He was slain while investigating the ambush of House Godendar's ruby caravan.
