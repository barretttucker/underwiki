jewelry

Darkelves of both sexes often adorn themselves in a wide variety of jewelry - necklaces, rings, earrings, lip rings, nose rings, bracelets, armbands, pins, headbands, etc. Darkelves are very skilled at the various crafts required to design and make very fine jewelry of all kinds. They often prize svirfneblin gemcutters, as no race is as good at drawing the best from each and every stone as they.

The only times that darkelves are not so decorated are when stealth is needed or combat is imminent. An unexpected meeting with unadorned darkelves would be very undesirable.
