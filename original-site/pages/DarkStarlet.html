DarkStarlet

Dark starlets represent the ultimate expression of the darker side of fame. They seek to use their spells and abilities to control and manipulate their audience solely to enhance their fame and dominance in life and their art. They can be haughty and arrogant to those they deem "below them" (anyone they have ever successfully intimidated or manipulated) and flattering and beguiling to their "betters" (anyone they haven't dominated yet or could improve their the starlet's status).

Inspiration: "When you're a beautiful supermodel and have millions of dollars, it's almost your obligation to destroy the lives of the ugly poor people around you." Maybe a darker version of The Charmer

Class Features

Distracting Presence

Due to their glamorous nature and dazzling aspect, Dark Starlets gain their Charisma bonus to their AC. They retain this bonus when flat footed. Though they loose this bonus if they are concealed from the attacker or behind cover.

Starlet Nature

Dark Starlets gain a bonus to diplomacy skill checks equal to their class level when dealing with their "betters", and a penalty to their diplomacy skill checks equal to their class level when deal with "the little people."

The converse is also true for intimidate skill checks, they get an equivalent bonus when dealing with "underlings" and a penalty when dealing with "patrons."
