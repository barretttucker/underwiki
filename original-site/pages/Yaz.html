Yaz

Commander of House Kenyrin's elite guard at Bhaerynden and trusted bodyguard of Illiam. Imbued by her master with an intense distrust of outsiders as well as an equally fierce loyalty to him. Regarded the Spider Guard as inferiors and foes from the moment she met them. Yaz was slain along with her entire contingent during an ambush in the catacombs before she could testify as to the circumstances of her master's death.
