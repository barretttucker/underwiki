Dinlin

Dinlin Oussund is a cleric of the Spider Queen, and has been assigned to Nerr'tenden.

The residents at the outpost cower from her unpredictable, iron hand and her whip. She spends most of her time either praying to Lolth or stalking the passages and chambers away from the stronghold’s walls. When she isn’t doing one of these things, she is likely berating or whipping one of the stronghold’s inhabitants.

Recently presumed deceased under mysterious circumstances, Dinlin's disappearance has sparked quite a fervor among the clergy.
