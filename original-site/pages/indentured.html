indentured

When a dark elf incurs significant debt to the house, they may become indentured (willingly or not).

The form of indenture is up to the judging house superior.

Some examples:

  • enslaved for a period of several years

  • forced to do menial labor or degrading tasks for own group or the superior

  • demoted

  • forced to repay inflated loan with interest in an impossibly short time
