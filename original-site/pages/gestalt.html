gestalt

Gestalt characters are outlined in the Unearthed Arcana book. The basic concept is that players can create a multi-class character that is a true combination of two classes. Each time you level, you get the benefits of both classes (e.g. spells and feats), and also take the more powerful improvement rate for saves, attacks, etc.

The downside is that you will suffer a significant experience point penalty; your character will count as 3 levels higher than actual when the DM calculates combat experience point awards. You can check out the http://www.d20srd.org website for more information - use the Encounter Calculator on the right.
