family

The Family of a house is the actual blood-born family whose name is used by the House - these are the nobility. Only those with the House Name can be promoted to positions of real leadership in the House. The Matriarch also has the authority to grant the House Name to other individuals in the House. This is only done under extremely rare situations, and is an irreversible decision (of course, she retains the authority to kill them if need be...).
