slaves

Dark Elves are intelligent and like to make the most of scarce resources - they often will capture and enslave opponents rather than kill them. Typical sources of slaves are: other Dark Elves from destroyed houses or members of other races who were foolish enough to resist Dark Elf development and expansion into local areas.

Slaves are kept as long as they are more productive than the cost of feeding them.
