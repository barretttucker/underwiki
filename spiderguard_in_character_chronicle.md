Zacxh picks up his pin, stuffs it unceremoniously into his pouch and grabs a loaf of sweat bread which he stuffs into his mouth with a swig of water to chase it down.

"By the way in answer to the question, I was captured enslaved and beaten by house Oosund. The priestesses of the house conspired and executed my demise. "I believe we are all `dead'. Perhaps others of you might look at this opportunity as I have?"

Zacxh returns to his corner and resumes his servants posture. Only now, his hand rests lightly on the pommel of his well oiled and polished rapier. His eyes again appear dead.
